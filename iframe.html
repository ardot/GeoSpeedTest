What do these numbers mean??

Internet speed is measure in Megabits per Second (Mbps). As you may know, all computer signals are sent in binary code, as 0s or 1s. Megabits per second is a measure of how many thousand 0s and 1s can be transmitted each second.

In this document, 10 Mbps is used as the division between fast (green) and slow (red). 10 Mbps is about the US average internet speed connection, and a rate that allows for consistent streaming of online video and other content. With a 10 Mbps connection, you can download a 1 Gigabyte (GB) movie in about 15 minutes.
